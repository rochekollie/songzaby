About Us

Songzaby podcast is a show about interviews, tips, and insights for independent artists on how to distribute, promote, and license their music to major music platforms.

The idea for the show was inspired by the our dedication to help independent artists and artists who are looking to promote their music with knowledge, tips, and insights from the industry leading professionals in the music industry.

The show is hosted by Songzaby and is available on SoundCloud and YouTube.

We publish our podcasts on iTunes, Google Play, and Spotify. The show is ad supported and is free to listen on all platforms. We also have a free podcast subscription on Podbean. If you would like to sponsor the show, appear as guest on the show, or learn more about what we do, please contact us.

Songzaby Podcast is owned and publish by Songzaby, Inc, a digital music distribution company. We are an independent commercial company organization and are not affiliated with any of the platforms mentioned above.
